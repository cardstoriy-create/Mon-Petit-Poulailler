## 1. Stylistic Objective
The goal is to produce "children's book" style illustrations that exude a warm, reassuring, and handcrafted atmosphere. Every image must look handmade on physical media.

---

## 2. The "Artist Grain" Protocol
To avoid a "smooth" digital look, every prompt must follow the aesthetic of **Reference-Style.png** and include:
* **Support:** Thick and visible watercolor paper grain texture.
* **Mediums:** A blend of soft watercolor washes and colored pencil hatching.
* **Outlines:** Fine, delicate, and slightly irregular lines (pencil-drawn look).
* **Keywords:** "Hand-drawn aesthetic", "Visible pencil strokes", "Watercolor grain".

---

## 3a. Family Identity (Character Consistency)
Characters must be strictly based on **Reference-Family.png** for physical traits:
* **Martin (The Father):** Benevolent silhouette, well-groomed full beard, kind eyes.
* **Julie (The Mother):** Wavy auburn hair, welcoming smile, protective posture.
* **Anna (The Daughter):** Characteristic twin pigtails, joyful expression.
* **Lucas (The Son):** Short hair, often curious and interacting with animals.

## 3b. Pets (Visual Consistency)

## 3c. Environment & Scenery (Visual Consistency)
Environmental elements must follow the design established in **Reference-Scenery.png**:
* **The House:** Cream-colored cottage with a terracotta tiled roof and blue shutters.
* **The Fence:** Natural wood picket fence with a matching gate (crucial for boundary scenes).
* **Garden Elements:** Raised wooden vegetable patches and round-foliage apple trees.
